Introducing
Real Estate Connect

The trusted platform designed to bridge the gap between the public and the real estate industry. In light of recent legal changes that limit the visibility of buyer's agent commission percentages, we created Real Estate Connect to foster honesty and transparency in the market.

Our platform allows verified real estate agents to post and search commission rates on sold properties, ensuring that both agents and the public have access to vital information that promotes fairness and informed decision-making. At Real Estate Connect, we believe that transparency is key to building trust and making real estate transactions clearer for everyone involved.
